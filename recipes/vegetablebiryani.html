Vegetable Biryani Recipe

Description

Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful rice dish that is popular across South Asia and the Middle East. This recipe features basmati rice layered with tender, spiced chicken, caramelized onions, and aromatic herbs, all cooked together to perfection.

Perfect for celebrations or a special weekend meal, biryani is loved for its vibrant colors, delicious taste, and rich aroma. Serve it hot with raita or a fresh salad for a truly memorable feast!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken pieces (or vegetables for a veg version)
  • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp biryani masala or garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • Whole spices (2-3 cloves, 2 cardamom pods, 1 bay leaf, 1 small cinnamon stick)
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 4 tbsp cooking oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Boil until 70% cooked, then drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan. Add whole spices and sliced onions. Sauté until onions are golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute. Add chicken pieces and cook until they start to brown.
  4. Stir in chopped tomatoes, yogurt, turmeric, biryani masala, and salt. Cook until the chicken is tender and oil separates.
  5. Add half of the chopped coriander and mint leaves. Layer the cooked rice over the chicken mixture.
  6. Top with remaining coriander and mint. Cover tightly and cook on low heat for 20 minutes (or use the dum method).
  7. Turn off the heat and let it rest for 10 minutes. Fluff gently with a fork before serving hot.
