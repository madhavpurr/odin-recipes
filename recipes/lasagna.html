Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish featuring layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. This hearty and satisfying casserole is perfect for family dinners and special occasions, offering a wonderful blend of flavors and textures.

With its golden, bubbly top and comforting taste, lasagna is sure to become a favorite at your table. Serve it with a crisp salad and garlic bread for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 2 cups marinara or tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil or parsley (for garnish, optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large pan. Sauté onion and garlic until soft. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg and half the Parmesan cheese.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a thin layer of meat sauce. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella, and more sauce. Repeat layers, finishing with sauce and the remaining cheeses on top.
  5. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 15 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Let lasagna cool for 10 minutes before slicing. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired and serve warm.
