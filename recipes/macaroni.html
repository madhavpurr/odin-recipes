Macaroni Creamy Recipe

Description

Macaroni Creamy is the ultimate comfort food! This recipe features tender macaroni pasta coated in a rich, velvety cream sauce made with cheese, milk, and a hint of garlic. It’s quick, satisfying, and perfect for busy weeknights or a cozy meal at home.

You can enjoy this creamy macaroni on its own or customize it by adding veggies, cooked chicken, or crispy bacon. It’s a crowd-pleaser for kids and adults alike!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups macaroni pasta
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup cream (optional for extra creaminess)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish, optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the macaroni pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté for 1 minute.
  3. Sprinkle in the flour and cook, stirring, for another minute to form a roux.
  4. Slowly whisk in the milk, ensuring there are no lumps. Cook until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Add grated cheese and cream, stirring until the cheese is fully melted and the sauce is smooth.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the sauce and toss until well coated.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley if desired.
